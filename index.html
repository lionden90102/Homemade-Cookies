Recipe

Dry Mixture

1.2 1/4 Cups ALL-Purpose Flour (270g)
2.1/4 tsp. Baking Soda
3.1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
4.1/2 tsp. Salt
5.1 Tbsp. Cornstarch
6.Mix

Mixing Mixture

7.1/2 cup unsalted Butter, room temperature (113g)
8.Mix For Cream
9.1 cup Granulated Sugar (200g) Wile Mixing
10.1 Egg. room temperature
11.1 1/2 tsp. Vanilla Extract (7.5mL)
12.Optional 1/4 tsp. Almond Extract
13.Stop Mixing
14.1/4 cup Sour Cream, room temperature (60g) Mix until in mixure
15.Put Dry Mixture In With Mining Mixture, Mix until all are together
16.Put on partmitsh papaer or plastic
17.Flaten the mixture
18.Roll out and put it in the frige for 1 Hour
19.Take out of frige
20.Put on something and put some flower on it and put the mixure on it then put some flower on the mixure
21.Roll it out
22.cut it in what ever
23.Put in oven for (375F) (8-9 Min)

Butter Cream Froting

25.2 cups Powdered Surgar (240g), Mix
26.2 Tbsp. Milk (30mL) plus more if needed, Mix wile doing it
